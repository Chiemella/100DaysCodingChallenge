Baby Lion.

Lion cubs have soft, fluffy fur that is usually a sandy brown color with dark spots.

Baby leopard

Leopard cubs are completely dependent on their mother for the first few months of their lives.

Baby Cheetah

Baby cheetahs have small, retractable claws, which they use to climb trees and play.

Baby Jaguar

Jaguar cubs are born with closed eyes and folded ears.

Baby Puma

Baby pumas are born weighing between 230 to 450 grams (8 to 16 ounces) and are about 30 centimeters (1 foot) in length.
